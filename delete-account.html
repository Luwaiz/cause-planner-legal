Cause Planner – Account Deletion Request

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account directly within the Cause Planner app:

1. Open the Cause Planner app
2. Go to the Account tab
3. Scroll down and tap Delete Account
4. Confirm the deletion when prompted

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your name and email address
  • All tasks, classes, notes, and goals
  • Study groups and calendar data
  • AI conversation history
  • Subscription and billing records (payment history is retained by Stripe per their policy)

Retention Period

All personal data is deleted within 90 days of your account deletion request. Some anonymized, non-identifiable data may be retained for legal compliance purposes.

Can't Access the App?

If you are unable to access your account, you can request deletion by emailing us directly:

Email: minatoventures@minatoventuresinc.com

Please include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 30 days.
